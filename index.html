Who am I and What I did

My name is Zeyi Ren, you can call me Rick. I'm one of the developers of the Zelda: The Legend of Zelda--Michigan Snow. I also individually crafted the game SneakOut.

Technology

C# and Unity enpowers these game.

Contribution

In the Zelda: The Legend of Zelda--Michigan Snow, I developed the Character movement and animation, created enemy Keese, Gel, boss Aquamentus and these crazy on-fire buddies. Brainstorm and design the brand new snowball weapon that can both roll and throw. In the recent gold version of SneakOut, I create a new mouse-control system--trajectory navigation system for both the player and its enemies. In the Paws and Claws, I designed and implemented the card selection mechanism. And also created the round of the game to make it multiple-round. I also implemented the random generation of the card. Additionally, I did some animation work including character animations and card description animations.
